About Us


We are StarBlitz #840
The FLL Team of HaKfar HaYarok, Israel
We inspire, empower and create change by using science, technology,
engineering, and mathematics as a multi-cultural language to connect
everyone, regardless of gender, age, race or religion.
FIRST has inspired us to take STEM education to the next level by
starting and mentoring teams in Israel and around the world.
Making a difference is not about building robots, it is about building
a better world, making a difference, and that's what we're all about.
